open caption screenings:

Rooftop Movies is proud to present sessions with Open Captions. Open Captions (OC) are text with sound description displayed on the screen to allow all patrons to enjoy movies together. This includes, but is not limited to, dialogue, music and sound effects. Check out the upcoming sessions with Open Captions below.
