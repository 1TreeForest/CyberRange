About Espedeo

Founded in 2006, Espedeo Holdings Limited (Espedeo) is a global digital cinema display equipment and solutions provider. We offer the premium commercial cinema projectors, cinema 3D system equipment and after-sales support service.

We take special pride in the brand and protect your investment with superior customer service, warranties and technical support.

Our product development philosophy aims to strike a balance among quality, simplicity, innovation and cost efficiency to help improve the motion picture exhibitors operational effectiveness and homeowners cinematic experience.

From engineers and software developers to product managers and a vast sales network, we employ hundreds of passionate people with 13 offices and 33 local service centers around the world.
