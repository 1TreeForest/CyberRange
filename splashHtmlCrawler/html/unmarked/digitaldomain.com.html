Features

arrow_drop_down

For Over 25 Years, Digital Domain’s Award-winning artists have delivered legendary visual effects for some of the most significant and challenging feature films ever produced.

Digital Domain lives at the intersection of technology and story, creating stunning visual effects that make the unimaginable possible. Our relentless passion for creating flawless visuals has earned Digital Domain nine Academy Award nominations and three wins for the films “The Curious Case Of Benjamin Button,” “Tron: Legacy,” and “Titanic.” Behind each of Digital Domain’s visionary breakthroughs and awards is a global team of deeply committed artists, technicians, and craftspeople.
