Introducing ONECard

ONECard is a smartphone application that will be used by students, faculty and staff members at Humber College and the University of Guelph-Humber as their digital identification, digital key and digital wallet solution for campus-wide services. It’s free, easy to use and secure. Visit the ONECard website to learn more about it.
