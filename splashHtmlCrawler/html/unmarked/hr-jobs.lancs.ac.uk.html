    Lancaster University is among the best in the UK. We are in the top 20 in all 3 UK league tables as well as being highly ranked in international league tables such as the QS World Rankings.

    We have an international reputation for excellence in research and teaching, attracting bright minds from all over the world. Our research culture bridges the gap between academia and business, and results in pioneering contributions to a variety of fields in the UK and beyond.

    The University was named International University of the Year 2020 by the Times and the Sunday Times.
