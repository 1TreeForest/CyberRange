VINELAND DRIVE-IN CUSTOMER RULES

The Drive-In will continue to be open nightly so that you can enjoy movies from the comfort and safety of your cars.  Below are rules that we require all moviegoers to follow.  They are intended to protect you, our employees and community as our collective health is of upmost importance during these challenging times.

·         Please do not enter the premises if you are experiencing flu like symptoms or have a fever, a persistent cough, experiencing shortness of breath or any other COVID-19 symptoms.  Please also do not enter the premises if you have been diagnosed with COVID-19 or are not vaccinated and have been exposed to someone who has been diagnosed with COVID-19.    

·         You are not required to wear a mask in your vehicle or when outdoors at the Drive-In.  You must wear a mask when visiting the bathroom and snack bar. 

·         Please continue to practice social distancing when moving about at the Drive-In.

·         You must follow all other guidelines posted at the property as well as CDC and LA County Department of Public Health guidelines. 

If you do not follow the above guidelines, you will be asked to leave without refund.  We encourage credit card transactions. Otherwise, please bring exact change.

We sincerely thank all our loyal customers for their continued support.
