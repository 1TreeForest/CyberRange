Chinese Government Scholarship Program

Program Introduction

For international students out of China only who want to study master or PhD degree can apply for HIT Independent Recruitment Program. It is one of the programs of Chinese Government Scholarship (hereinafter referred to as CSC scholarship) and assigned from Ministry of Education (hereinafter referred to as MOE) to the university to recruit outstanding international students. Applicants need to submit the application materials according to school requirements. HIT will select the best applicants and recommend to the China Scholarship Council.

China Discovery Program (CDP)

Program Introduction

Aiming to provide international students with an accessible and high-quality platform to understand China, HIT invites excellent university staff, international experts, famous enterprise CEOs to offer a portfolio of English-medium courses that include the social sciences, Chinese culture, Chinese economy and business, interdisciplinary studies and many others. In addition to the intensive courses, we also offer you online activities, like paper cutting, Chinese cooking etc.

Short-term Chinese Language Program

Program Introduction

The short term course held by HIT in winter and summer are very unique and practical in features, with various and plentiful Chinese cultural learning activities and individualized costumed made elements. It is deeply welcomed by many international students. The academy would take many requirements and opinion from students into account and would then perfectly blend both the language learning and cultural experience into the course. Therefore allowing students who are new to Chinese easily and completely embedded themselves into the Chinese culture in order to raise the level of Chinese promptly and experience Chinese cultural more effectively.

Long-term Chinese Language Program

Program Introduction

College of International Education (CIE)，HIT provides the best value for standard Mandarin Chinese language instruction with competitive tuition, affordable living expenses, and highly qualified experienced teachers in a supportive and comfortable learning environment. At present, there are nearly 50 highly qualified and experienced full-time and part-time teachers in CIE. Their rich teaching experience, advanced teaching concept, flexible teaching methods and enthusiasm attract thousands of International students to HIT to learn Chinese.
