Welcome to the sparkling new global Cultural-Retail destination — K11 MUSEA.

With a name inspired by the Muses in Greek mythology, K11 MUSEA aspires to enrich your daily life through the power of creativity, culture and innovation. Located in the heart of the Victoria Dockside art and cultural district, this world-class experiential landmark brings in immersive experiences in retail, art, culture, entertainment and gastronomy, all under one roof.

28 Jan - 27 Mar 2022
Adorable or Not? Stickymonger’s Giant Strawberry Questions Reality

Brooklyn-based Korean artist Stickymonger (also known as Joohee Park) brings her two-dimensional work to life for the first time with her giant strawberry art installation seriesStrawberry Stalkerat K11 MUSEA! See her floating strawberries in the Opera Theatre now!

A multi-disciplinary artist who developed her obsession with black, sticky fluidity from playing with oil drums next to her family’s gas station as a child, Stickymonger’s work is a representation of her imaginative universe.

WithStrawberry Stalker, Stickymonger asks the question: at what point in your life did you decide strawberries are cute? To her, there’s nothing cute about strawberries; they just are.

Living in this digital day and age — constantly being exposed to and overstimulated by ideas, opinions and thoughts wherever we turn — we become more susceptible than ever to conditioned thinking and behaviour. To Stickymonger, the question of whether or not strawberries are cute was a gateway to something bigger — that we’ve been conditioned to think that way — the very inspiration behind theStrawberry Stalkerseries.

To commemorate the giant strawberry installation, Stickymonger has designed a series of collectable Strawberry Crystal Ball blind boxes, with a limited 500 units each available in black, red, pink and white, exclusively on K11 Design Store eSHOP and on K11 ESHOP. The black and white colourways are specially made to celebrate this momentous collaboration with K11 MUSEA.

Peer into the world of Stickymonger’s giant strawberry now at K11 MUSEA!

Dates: 28 Jan 2022 - 27 Mar 2022
Location: G/F, Opera Theatre
Opening Hours: 11:00am-9:00pm
K11 Event
From 5 February
NIGO’s KENZO 2022 Collection Launches Exclusively at K11 MUSEA

Much anticipated since his appointment as KENZO’s Artistic Director, Japanese streetwear legend, music producer and fashion designer NIGO struck the world with his recently unveiled ‘Real-to-Wear’ genderless wardrobe, the presentation of which took place at the Galerie Vivienne — the same place where Kenzo Takada held his first fashion show back in 1970 (coincidentally, the same year NIGO was born).

Here, NIGO himself also wore a sneak preview of the previously unshown limited edition drop. Two five-piece wardrobes of the collection for men and women are centred on the boke, a Japanese flower that blooms in February, which has also become part of the new KENZO aesthetic. Discover navy-hued jackets, crew-neck sweatshirts, jersey cardigans, long-sleeved T-shirts, oversized hoodies, cargo trousers and skirts, and blankets for the drop all donning the new motif.

Only the second Japanese designer to helm the brand since Takada himself, NIGO went back to KENZO’s roots, re-examining house codes alongside his personal experience in the fashion industry to develop a collection that threads KENZO’s inherent cultural language into each piece. Synthesising Japanese traditions with Western ideals, NIGO builds a continuous brand story that captures KENZO’s heritage while paving a way to the future.

Launching on 5 February — or “ni” (2) “go” (5) corresponding to his name in Japanese — see the first limited edition drop for Spring/Summer 2022 exclusively available at K11 MUSEA!

HOUSE OF MUSEA: A Grand English Estate Embracing the Season of New Beginnings

Celebrate the season of love and new beginnings as K11 MUSEA transforms into a romantic English estate — the HOUSE OF MUSEA — reminiscent of grand garden parties once held to exchange inspiration, creativity and conversation over delectable confections!

Garden Pop-up
Visit our House to soak up the splendour of our picturesque Garden Pop-up and get lost in the world of a grand garden party affair. Plunge into the lavish greenery offered by fanciful hedges and topiaries, and beds of romantic florals, as you enjoy a flurry of creativity exchange with a fascinating array of workshops hosted by the K11 Kulture Academy.

Stay tuned for our lineup of weekend pop-ups purveying handcrafted treats and arts and crafts, all celebrating the romantic season of spring!

Date: From 28 January onwards
Location: 6F, K11 MUSEA

Strawberry Stalker by Stickymonger
Join us as K11 MUSEA indulges in springtime strawberry fever, bringing two monumental strawberry artworks by Brooklyn-based Korean artist Stickymonger to the Opera Theatre — a thoughtful installation questioning the very idea of originality and uniqueness with the artist’s seemingly adorable strawberry character.
Date: From 28 January onwards
Location: G/F Opera Theatre

Strawberry Fever at K11 MUSEA
Embark on a strawberry-infused journey where the luscious red fruit is interpreted in various delectable ways — from the traditional Wimbledon must-have of Strawberries and Cream, to luxurious afternoon tea sets, illusion cakes, freshly baked pastries and artistic coffees. Get ready to be swept away in the world of heavenly strawberry treats!
Date: From 28 February onwards
