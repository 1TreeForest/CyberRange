Welcome To The VIFF Centre 

Celebrate cinema on the big screen in our luxurious Vancity Theatre and our new Studio Theatre. Don't forget to check out the great selection of films streaming now on VIFF Connect.

All year long, our VIFF curators bring film fans some of the best cinema and talks from the world's most creative voices. Stay in the loop, sign up for VIFF News to get our weekly listings of films and events... you won't want to miss out!
