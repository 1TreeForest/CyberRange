The Lost Boys

Friday, October 29, 2021
6:30pm

Enjoy a screening of The Lost Boys movie on the beach in front of the arcades! Santa Cruz and the Boardwalk are forever linked to this cult classic and there’s nothing like watching it right where it was filmed with the smell of corn dogs and salt water taffy filling the air.
