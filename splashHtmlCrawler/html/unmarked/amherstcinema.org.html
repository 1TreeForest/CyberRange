The Runner
THE RUNNER follows a strange woman as she travels to a secluded, rural town where her violent compulsions are slowly revealed. The story intertwines with band Boy Harsher performing on a public access channel. Their music scores the strange woman's descent deeper into the unknown. Boy Harsher will join us for an in-person post screening Q&A!
