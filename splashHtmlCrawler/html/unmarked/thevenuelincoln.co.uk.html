Welcome to The Venue

Now that our cinema programme has finished for the year, we wanted to offer an update on the future of The Venue.

Following on from recent global changes amongst the entertainment industry, Bishop Grosseteste University has taken the opportunity to review our current commercial offer and venue.

Therefore, we have made the decision to temporarily suspend our operation at this time whilst we strategically review our business.

In the meantime, please keep a look out for The Venue updates.

– The Venue Team
