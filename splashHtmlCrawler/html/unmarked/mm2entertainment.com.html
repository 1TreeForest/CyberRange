Our company takes a serious view of such falsehoods. We are currently investigating the matter and will take all necessary follow-up actions.

Meanwhile, the public is advised to contact us immediately for verification if they are approached by any person/s or company/ies representing themselves as our authorised business partners, agents, or official representatives for any business transaction involving the mm2 Group of Companies to avoid being fraudulently deceived in any way.
