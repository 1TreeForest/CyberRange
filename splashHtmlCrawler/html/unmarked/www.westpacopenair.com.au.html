the experience

The buzz starts from early evening as friends, colleagues and lovers gather to enjoy the event’s harbourside bar and restaurant. With stunning views over the Sydney Opera House, Harbour Bridge and the city skyline, the event offers an amazing festive atmosphere that captures the spirit of Sydney in summertime.

As night falls, an unrivalled cinematic experience unfolds. Our giant 350sqm screen rises from Sydney Harbour to stand three-storeys-tall before an eagerly awaiting audience. With state-of-the-art Dolby Digital® surround sound, comfortable seating for all and a fabulous selection of films, we create the world’s most beautiful cinema.
