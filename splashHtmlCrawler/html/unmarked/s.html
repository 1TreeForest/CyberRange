Welcome to Sisters Movie House

Sisters Movie House re-opened in May 2021 after a 14-month absence, thanks to Covid-19. Under the orders of the Governor, as of Friday August 13th 2021, masks are once again required until further notice. We remain free to sell 100% of our seating capacity, although in practice, this rarely happens. Should you have any concerns, please feel free to speak with a member of our team. Sisters Movie House adheres to the national standards outlined at CinemaSafe.

Opening Hours
