Policies

EQUIPMENT AUTHORISATION RULES
影视器材使用权限管理规定

1. All reservations except Final-Year Projects reservations must be made via Web Checkout’s self-service “Connect 2” system. Connect 2 can be accessed at https://livsofta.getconnect2.com/ 
所有器材（除毕业作品所需器材）必须通过“Connect 2”线上自助系统进行预订。Connect 2系统的访问链接为：https://livsofta.getconnect2.com/ 

2. Online reservation must be made at least 1 workday before checkout.
必须在器材借出前一个工作日进行线上预订。 

3. Equipment reservations will be categorized as follows;
PRACTICE – Out with agreed official scheduled project shoot times, students will be able to reserve equipment for a maximum of 1 day for practice.

ALLOCATED PROJECT SHOOT DATES – When working on an official School project whether for assessment or for agreed corporate/collaborative work, students can reserve equipment for the following amount of time;

Fiction Film - A maximum 4 days at one time.

Documentary Film - A maximum of 7 days at one time.

In exceptional circumstances the maximum length of a reservation can be extended if approved by the Creative Director or Dean of School.

器材借用期限将按如下分类:

实践 – 除作业项目外，学生可借用器材进行实践，一次最多借1天；

指定项目 – 为作业项目或已授权的课外项目借用器材，借用期限如下：

故事片 – 一次最多借4天；

纪录片 – 一次最多借7天。

特殊情况下，经院长或创意总监的同意后，器材借用期限可延长。

4. All reservation extensions require at least 1 workday advance notice and must be arranged with the Equipment Manager in person or over the phone.
如需延期归还器材，请至少提前1个工作日通过电话或当面与工作人员沟通协调。

5. Reservations are held past pick-up time for 30 minutes. After this time the reservation is automatically canceled and the equipment is made available to other students. 
订单将按规定领取时间保留30分钟，超时将自动取消。

6. Please bring the signed Equipment Loan Agreement Form (authorised and signed by the module leader or Program Director) for checking out.
领取器材时，需携带纸质器材借用协议书（由授课教师或专业主任签字批准用于此课程）。

7. Upon arrival at the Equipment Storeroom, it is the student or staff’s responsibility to review the specified return time, thoroughly check the provided equipment list, confirm all items listed are present and in working order, then sign the Equipment Loan Agreement Form.
领取器材时，借用人需仔细检查器材，确认清单上的所有器材均已提供并可以正常使用，查看并确认规定的归还时间。所有信息确认无误后签署器材借用协议书。

8. Student/staff should alert Equipment Storeroom staff if anything is missing, unusual, extra, or malfunctioning during their equipment check.
在领取器材时，若发现任何器材遗失、使用异常或故障，或发现未在订单里的器材，需及时告知工作人员。

9. Take note of how the equipment is packed; the equipment must be returned in the same organization.
领取器材时注意器材的包装方式，归还时需保持原样。

10. The student/staff signing the Equipment Loan Agreement is solely responsible for loss and/or damage to, and prompt return of the equipment, and will be charged accordingly. 
签署器材借用协议书的学生/教职工对器材负有全责，若器材损失、损坏、或未按时归还，签署协议的学生将获相应处罚。

11. Equipment should not be left in labs, teaching rooms or any other space. If any equipment is left unattended in a room it will be removed and returned to the equipment storeroom.
器材不得遗留在机房、教室或其它房间内，无人看管的器材将被收回到器材室。

12. Booked equipment should not be given to other individuals.
不得将器材转借给他人使用。

13. If a problem arises with the equipment during checkout period, please notify the Equipment Storeroom staff immediately of the nature of the issue. Do not attempt repairs of equipment yourself.  
如器材使用期间出现问题，必须立即将问题反映给工作人员，不得自行处理。

14. Equipment must be returned by the specified return time determined by the student/staff and confirmed upon pick-up. It is also required to return all equipment on the same order at one time. 
必须在应还时间归还器材，并一次性归还同一订单的所有器材。

15. In-class project reservations should be made on Connect 2 by the module leader at least one workday prior to the class. The equipment should only be used during class. The selected checkout and checkin time online should be the same as the actual needed time. Students or the module leader, whoever pick up the equipment should sign the Equipment Loan Agreement Form. 
课堂使用的器材应由任课教师至少提前一个工作日在线上预订，器材仅供课堂使用。实际取还时间应与线上预定的取还时间一致。领取器材的学生或教师需要填写器材借用协议书。

16. For Final-Year Project, all equipment lists must be authorised by the faculty advising the project. Please make all reservations at least TWO WEEKS in advance via email. Equipment requests should be complete when submitted to the Equipment Storeroom. Additions made at the time of pickup may need to be fulfilled at a later time. Each shoot will have one designated producer who will make all equipment reservations under their name.
毕业作品器材清单由指导教师授权，并至少提前两周通过邮件预订，如临时增加器材将无法保证按时提供。项目需指定一位制片人来负责所有器材预订。

17. If students, faculty and staff want to travel with SoFTA owned equipment, it requires at least 2 workdays advance notice to the Equipment Manager, in order to apply insurance. Otherwise the insurance won’t cover any equipment damage during the trip. Please also write down the destination on the Equipment Loan Agreement while checkout. 
如需携带器材长途旅行，请至少提前2个工作日申请，以便办理保险相关事宜，否则保险概不理赔在途中任何因意外造成的设备损坏。请同时在领取器材时在器材借用协议书上填写目的地。

 

PENALITIES

处罚规定


1. Penalties include replacement/repair charges, and loss of all borrowing privileges for the rest of the semester. When a student or staff member is assessed as responsible for loss or damage, they are provided one week to make a payment, appeal, or set up a payment plan. After one week, their account is placed on hold and equipment privileges are suspended. 
处罚包括维修、更换，以及限制借用器材。当学生/教职工被认定为器材丢失/损坏的责任人时，将有一周的时间进行赔付、申诉或制定赔付计划。若一周内未赔付，账户将被暂停，器材借用权限也将被取消。


2. Penalties will be enforced on the following:
Late return of equipment
Broken equipment 
Loss of equipment or individual components (e.g. screws, springs, tie-downs, etc.)
Improper, careless handling or repacking of equipment (including shooting in foul weather or in sandy environments)
以下行为将受到处罚：

延迟归还器材
损坏器材
丢失器材或配件（如：螺丝、弹簧、固定装置等）
不当使用、搬运和包装器材（包括在恶劣天气或沙质环境中使用器材）

3. In the case of loss or damage of equipment, please refer to the University’s Fixed Asset Management Policy and Insurance Policy.
若器材丢失或损坏，需根据学校的固定资产管理政策及保险政策进行赔偿或接受处罚。


4. Late Penalty — Equipment returned after 5pm（except for In-Class Projects） on the due date will be considered as late and the account will be placed on hold and equipment checkout is suspended.
Late 1 day – 1 week hold; 
Late 2 days – 2 weeks hold; 
Late 3 days – 3 weeks hold;
And so forth.

延迟归还处罚：除课上使用的器材外，晚于应还日期当日17点归还的器材将视为延迟归还。若延迟归还，账户会被停用，器材不能借取。

延迟1天 – 禁止借用一周
延迟2天 – 禁止借用两周
延迟3天 – 禁止借用三周
               以此类推。

5. Cleaning Fee – Equipment used in sandy, wet, or otherwise adverse environments may require cleaning service. In such cases, payment should be made by the student/staff.
清洁费：在沙尘、潮湿或其它不利环境中使用器材会对器材造成损坏，如产生清洁费用，将由借用人承担。
