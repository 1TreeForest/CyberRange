Welcome to the Chinese Movie Database, the Chinese language movies and TV programmes database. This site has three versions, in Simplified Chinese, Traditional Chinese, and English, covering production, cast and crew, awards and biographies, reviews and books, and many other information of Chinese language movies made in mainland China, Hong Kong, Taiwan, and other regions. Starting from 2006, the Database also includes TV programmes. In total there are 16563 feature films, 509 TV programmes, 18453 unique individules, and 1204 companies in the Database (statistics).
