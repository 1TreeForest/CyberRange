6 Best Movie Torrent Sites To Use In 2021 [100% Working]

With the rise of streaming services, torrent sites are becoming obsolete over the years. Another reason behind the disappearance of torrent sites is the stringent laws by authorities.

Still, there exist some best torrent websites that you can use to download files via P2P torrenting technology. Here, we have listed the 6 best movie torrent sites that still work in 2021 and can be used to download movies via torrents.

Best Movie Torrent Sites 2021

1. The Pirate Bay – Best Movie Torrent Site

The Pirate Bay is undoubtedly one of the most popular movie torrent sites that you can come across on the internet. On this torrent site for movies, you can download Hollywood, Bollywood, and movies regional language via torrenting. TPB has a very intuitive interface and you can also refine your search using filters.

2. 1337x – Most Popular Torrent Site

1337x, also known as LEET, bags the second position in this list. This torrent site specializes in movies and besides hosting torrents, you can also watch trailers of the latest movies here. This torrent website offers options like Oscar Award winners, libraries, and the latest episode to further make itself useful for movie lovers.

3. EZTV – One Of The Oldest Movie Sites

Established back in 2005, EZTV is considered one of the oldest movie torrent sites that you can still use in 2021. This site is much more than just a website for downloading torrents. EZTV has a very active community where you can meet like-minded people. Besides movies, EZTV is also popular for hosting tons of TV shows. Here, you can download the latest movies and TV shows via torrenting.

4. Rarbg – Best Torrent Site For Latest Movies

If you want to watch the latest movies online, Rarbg should be your destination torrent site. One of the reasons why we have included Rarbg in this list of best movie torrent sites is the sheer number of movies available here.

Also, the website has been neatly designed with a dedicated section for movies and TV shows. On the homepage, you can also find a list of other torrent genres.

5. YTS – Best Torrent Site For Movies & TV Shows

YTS or Yify Movies is a torrent website dedicated to downloading movie torrents. It has been around for a long time now and has been quite popular amongst movie lovers. YTS’s biggest advantage is its interface and the number of filters it offers to refine your search.

While searching for movie torrents, you can apply filters such as quality, genre, language, year, and rating. The website has a large collection of movie torrents and we’re sure you’ll not be disappointed by it.

6. Torrentz2 – Get Millions of Torrents

Torrentz2 or previously Torrentz is a well-known torrent site that hosts a large number of torrents pertaining to movies, games, software, apps, and more.

Here, you can find a large number of movie torrents as this website does not host its own content but indexes torrent files from other websites. Therefore, you can consider it as a Google for movie torrents. However, Torrentz2 is riddled with ads and this might be a deal-breaker for some.

Choosing The Best Torrent Site For Movies

You can choose any torrent website from the list of the above-mentioned sites. We have personally checked all the websites enlisted here and we can assure you that these sites are 100% working.

However, we want to warn our readers that you might come across torrent files offering copyrighted content on these movie torrent sites. Therefore, we advise you to proceed with caution and download only verified torrent files or files with the highest number of seeders and leechers.

FAQs

1. What are the best torrent sites?

Some of the best torrent sites that you can visit in 2021 are The Pirate Bay, Kickass Torrent, 1337x, EZTV, and Extratorrent. These sites host a large number of torrent files for you to download.

2. How do I unblock torrent sites from my location?

If your ISP has blocked access to torrent sites, you can use them via proxy sites. You can visit blocked sites easily by using torrent proxy sites to enjoy downloading files via the P2P method.
