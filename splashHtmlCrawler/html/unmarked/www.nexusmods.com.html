简介

为你的星露谷新增一个叫做鹊的自订NPC，包括15个事件、200+新对话以及3个新菜谱，还有更多等你去探索。鹊暂住在哈维的诊所，他来自神秘的远东，之前是一名个人探险家。你觉得他看上去相当健康，但随着了解，你发现了他的秘密...
*预警*：深究起来这个故事没有真正意义上的he，介意慎玩
喜好

最爱：茶叶，绿茶，黄水仙，龙井虾仁，皮蛋瘦肉粥，糖醋鲤鱼
讨厌：一切垃圾和建材
喜欢：一切吃的，古物，矿物等
不喜欢：其他全民最爱的礼物比如五彩碎片等。但是你们可以送一个看看他的反应。
电影小食：
最爱：茉莉花茶
讨厌：乔家的东西
电影喜好：
最爱：恐怖片
讨厌：爱情片
行程

常规行程：8点起床，站在诊所的床边；10点前往博物馆开始工作直到下午4点，然后前往酒吧，待到9点回诊所，在床边玩手机直到11点睡觉
每周二：上午9点前往探险家公会，下午5点回诊所
每周三：上午照常，下午2点前往维克多家看书，下午6点回诊所
每周五：上午照常：下午2点前往维克多家借用他的电脑，下午5点回诊所
每周六：上午9点前往杂货店买东西，12点前往博物馆看书，下午4点回诊所，晚上9点睡觉
每周日：上午9点半在诊所治疗，下午2点前往卡洛琳的阳光房，8点回诊所
下雨：一整天都在诊所
夏季2号（生日）：上午10点前往海滩，晚上8点回诊所

事件（注：都要看过上一个事件才能触发下一个）

0心：早上9点到晚上9点之间进入诊所
2心1：看过SVE版本的加入探险家公会事件后，当鹊在探险家公会时前往（事件增益：所有武器伤害量大幅提升）
2心2：不下雨时且鹊在镇上的时候进入小镇（事件收获：学会菜谱龙井虾仁。如果这次选项错过，那么可以等鹊8心好感时在格斯那里购买菜谱）
4心：下午5点到晚上11点之间进入酒吧
6心1：下雨时任意时间前往沙滩
6心2：任意时间进入探险家公会（领取任务：伤痕）
6心3：鹊在诊所的时候前往诊所，如果你在诊所以外的地方把任务物品交了，鹊会再给你，带着物品去诊所，无所谓鹊在不在（领取任务：神秘玉镯，把任务物品交给法师完成任务）
7心：上午9点到下午5点之间前往诊所
8心1：上午9点到下午5点间前往诊所
8心2：上午9点到下午5点间前往森林
8心3：上午9点到下午5点间前往海滩
8心4：上午9点到下午5点间前往北部山区（温泉那一块）
8心5：完成8心4自动播放
10心：天气晴朗时，上午9点到下午5点间前往森林
14心：天气晴朗时，在上午6点10分到下午5点间进入农舍
作者的话

什么，终于轮到我唠叨的环节了？yes！感谢所有下载我家小喜鹊的人，这儿是天微。这是我第一次做自订NPC，说实话真的很艰难，每天都在崩溃边缘摸索，用了一个月的时间终于做出来了。
因为缺乏经验，作品里可能会出现事件比较粗糙等问题，如果你发现了任何错误，请立刻告诉我~
非常感谢K老师Krayon，没有K老师的指导和陪伴小喜鹊是搓不出来的，当时我跟K老师承诺一定会把她放在感谢列表第一个，我现在兑现承诺啦！K老师家的马修也很可爱很帅大家走过路过看一眼~
以及感谢贴吧的麻糬君的教程，没有这份教程我真的两眼一抹黑。希望更多的人能跟随麻糬的教程做出更好的作品。
我本人是代码零基础，画画也是最近才接触的板绘，很多不足之处我自己也明白，希望下一次能带来更好的作品，当然，我还是很喜欢我的小喜鹊w
以及N网评论区留不了中文，有什么问题如果不想用英语请去贴吧跟我反映！
感谢 @Anoukiekoukie 帮忙做的英文校对
感谢看到这里的你，祝你游戏愉快~
                                                     ——天微 2021.10.6

Introduction

Add a new custom NPC named Magpie(Que) into your Stardew Valley.Including 15 events,200+ dialogue and 3 new recipes.More things waiting for you to explore.
Magpie temporarily lived in the Hospital.He came from the mysterious Far Eastern. And he was a personal adventurer once before.You found somethings quiet weird cause that he looked extremely healthy through his appearance.But he do had secret hide behind and you need to discovered it.And maybe saved him?
*WARNING*：This story does not have a truly happy ending,if you mind it,you can stop now.

Gift Tastes

love：Tea Leaves，Green Tea，Daffodil，Congee with Minced Pork and Preserved Egg，Longjing Shrimp，sweat and sour carp
Hate：Trash.Who the heck on the earth will like them.
Like：Food,crops,Artifacts and Minerals
Dislike：All the universal-loved things,like prismatic shard for example.But you can give one to him and see his reaction.
Concession and Movie Tastes：：
Love：Jasmine Tea
Hate：All joja things
Movies：
love：horror film
dislike：romance film
Schedule

defalt：wake up at 8:00,go to ArchaeologyHouse at 10:00,work till 16:00 and then go to saloon,go back to hospital at 21:00,sleep at 23:00.
Every Tuesday：Go to AdventureGuild at 9:00,back to hospital at 17:00.
Every Wednesday：Things go to defalt befor noon，go to Victor's house at 14:00,back to hospital at 18:00.
Every Friday：Things go to defalt befor noon,go to Victor's house at 14:00,back to hospital at 17:00.
Every Saturday：Go to seedshop at 9:00，go to ArchaeologyHouse at 12:00，back to hospital at 16:00,sleep at 21:00.
Every Sunday：Stay at the hospital before noon,go to Caroline's sunroom at 14:00,back to hospital at 20:00.
rain：Stay at the hospital the whole day.
Summer 2（birthday）：Go to the beach at 10:00，back to hospital at 20:00.

Events Guild（ps：You have to see the previous event to trigger the next one）

0heart：Enter the hospital between 9 a.m. and 9 p.m
2hearts1：When you'd been a member of Adventurerguild,go to Adventurerguild when Magpie is there.(All weapons' harm would be rised after this event)
2hearts2：Enter the town when Magpie is there and it's not raining.（You will learn a new recipes after seen this event.If you chose the wrong choice and missed it,you can still buy it from Gus when your rlationship with Magpie had reached 8hearts.）
4hearts：Enter the saloon between 17 p.m and 23 p. m
6hearts1：Go to the beach when it's raining.
6hearts2：Enter the Adventurerguild at anytime. （Add quest:scar）
6hearts3：Go to the hospital when Magpie is there.If you gave the quest object to Magpie outside the hospital ,he will give it back to you.Then you need to go to the hospital with this object,otherwise you cannot start this event.（Add new quest：mysterious jade bracelet，found the wizard to complete it.）
7hearts：Go to the hospital between 9 a.m. and 5 p.m
8hearts1：Go to the hospital between 9 a.m. and 5 p.m
8hearts2：Go to the forest between 9 a.m. and 5 p.m
8hearts3：Go to the beach between 9 a.m. and 5 p.m
8hearts4：Go to the railroad between 9 a.m. and 5 p.m
8hearts5：Autoplayed after seen 8hearts4
10hearts：When the weather is sunny, go to the forest between 9 a.m. to 5 p.m
14hearts：When the weather is sunny, enter the farmhouse between 6:10 a.m. to 5 p.m

Author's words

Finally,time for me to say something!Hello everyone,I'm Adelaide,and this is my very first mod.Thank you for downloading my mod.I mean ,a mod originally made by me,not translation of others.It's really hard,I have to say,but I finished it after all.

Thanks a lot to @Anoukiekoukie for proofreading the English version!
And thank you for reading,have a nice game time~
