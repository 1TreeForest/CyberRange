    EXCEPTIONAL TITLE AND ESCROW SERVICES WITH A LOCAL TOUCH

    Founded in 2001, CW Title and Escrow is the largest title and escrow company headquartered in Washington. We provide exceptional title services for King, Pierce, Snohomish, Thurston and Spokane counties and we work with our affiliates to service title needs in other counties. In addition, we offer escrow services for all counties in the State of Washington.

    While we’re backed by two large, national underwriting companies, our decisions are made locally in Washington. Our in-house underwriting, title examiners, and escrow teams are experts in local properties and neighborhoods as they call this beautiful part of the US their home too. Our vast, local experience and passion for this industry allow us to overcome challenges that may arise from difficult title and escrow issues. Our local expertise allows you to efficiently move from under agreement to closing.
