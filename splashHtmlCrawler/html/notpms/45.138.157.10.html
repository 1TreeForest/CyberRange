Erik, the Asian Movie Enthusiast presents: A review of “Drug War”, a crime thriller from 2012 that was a Chinese/Hong Kong co-production directed by Johnnie To that stars Louis Koo, Sun Honglei and Huang Yi. I mentioned this director a bunch of times in my DVD Collection Playlist because I have some of his films in my collection. But I do not think I’ve discussed any of his films in a separate review on this YouTube Channel. That will change in 2019. One of my goals this year is to cover more of Johnnie To’s movies, with a focus on some of his more impressive titles. “Drug War” is one of the highlights of his career, in my opinion. The opening scene of this movie is a drug bust involving a bus-load of passengers who have consumed pouches of powdered material to smuggle across the border. After the bust, a cop is monitoring some of the criminals in a hospital, when Louis Koo’s character shows up after getting into a car accident. After it’s discovered that he has traces of drugs on him, he agrees to assist the police in an undercover operation that is intended to take down some big time criminals. This is a fantastic thriller that is one of my favorites from the last decade.
