The Alexander Advantage

Alexander Building Construction Co. was founded in 1928 and has been continuously operating in Central Pennsylvania for the past 92 years. Alexander's success stems not from short-term gains, but from long-term relationships founded on the premise of exceptional, comprehensive construction management services led by strong values and complete customer satisfaction.

In 2003, Alexander joined The Butz Family of Companies, and is now a member of one of the largest, most reputable construction management firms headquartered in Pennsylvania. The Butz Family of Companies is comprised of three operating entities: Alexander Building Construction Co. (Central PA), Shoemaker Construction Co. (Greater Philadelphia), and Alvin H. Butz, Inc. (Lehigh Valley).

Together we are able to provide construction management and contracting services in Pennsylvania, New Jersey and Delaware. We are consistently ranked among the nation's Top 400 General Contractors by ENR, McGraw-Hill's construction industry magazine. The value of construction managed by Butz Enterprises, Inc. in the past three years has averaged approximately $293 million.

The Butz Family of Companies Mission/Vision

The Butz Family of Companies is dedicated to superior project delivery in an atmosphere of teamwork and open communication in all of our business relationships. We will build on our tradition of excellence with an unwavering commitment to quality and continuous improvement. We will always perform with integrity, honesty, reliability, and enthusiasm. We will ensure growth opportunities for our employees and a continued commitment to our community.
